Conscious Trade means producers, distributors and consumers are empowered to shape and improve the system together, in a way that levels the playing field for farmers, preserves rainforests, and strengthens local farming communities.

Introducing Origin X Change: Yellow Seed’s marketplace partner

Origin X Change is the Conscious Cacao Marketplace built by Yellow Seed. It is a community-driven marketplace for cacao growers, service intermediaries and buyers to connect and trade with one another. Deeply rooted in the exchange of trust, Origin X Change provides a high level of transparency for buyers, and the ability to find products that contribute to the impact they care about.

Yellow Seed has partnered with Origin X Change to operate the marketplace, so it can focus on expanding the global Conscious Trade movement.

We track the collective impact of Conscious Trade

One of our core commitments is highlighting the impact of Conscious Trade on farmers, their communities and the environment. As the Conscious Trade network grows, Yellow Seed's visual and analytic tools aim to paint a portrait of the whole network, pointing to ways we can work better together.

The Farmer Choice Fund is a powerful way to support farmer-directed projects

The fund allows farmers to decide what impact is needed, helps them set goals and targets, and enables buyers to track how the prices they pay contribute to that impact. We are piloting the Farmer Choice Fund with Co-op ACCC in Peru, helping them raise $3000 to expand their unique agroforestry system, preserve biodiversity, ensure food security, and increase resilience to climate change.
