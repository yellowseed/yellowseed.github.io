Your donation supports:

  • Farmer equity: Provides ecologically and socially conscious farmers a voice and access to trade opportunities that value their knowledge, and impact on their land and communities.
  • Education and Stakeholder Engagement: Drives awareness of the Conscious Trade movement by educating the public on the benefits of Conscious Trade. Engages stakeholders (cacao buyers, cacao producers and NGO’s) in dialogue around how they can work together to accelerate the Conscious Trade movement.
  • Farmer Choice Fund: Operates the Farmer Choice Fund - a powerful way to support farmer-directed projects and action towards social and environmental impact. For this fund, farmers receive an additional 10% on the price they are paid for their beans, and Yellow Seed will facilitate the fund by aiding farmers in deciding what impact is needed, setting goals and targets, and reporting on impact.
