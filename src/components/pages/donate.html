Your donation supports:

  • Giving more farmers a voice and access to better trade options, along with a better understanding of their value in the market and fair prices.
  • Showcasing the stories and impact of progressive farmers and industry leaders working together to move beyond the level of compliance.
  • Building a conscious trade network based on wholeness, where everyone that is a part of the cacao supply chain can add products or services of value and contribute to making it better.

And this is just the beginning! We plan to add more features and scale to other products like coffee, tea, spices and dried fruit.

In 2015, 43% of our yearly budget was spent on operations, 33% on web design & coding, 12% on marketing, fundraising & outreach, and 12% strategy & design. Our 2016 operating budget is based on ongoing fundraising and is similarly allocated.
